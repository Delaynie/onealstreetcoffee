“Coffee, lemonade, and lasting impressions — always on O’Neal Street.”

Hi, we’re the family behind O’Neal Street Coffee — born and raised right here in Northwest Arkansas. Our name comes from my mom’s side of the family. My grandma O’Neal, was one of those people who left an impression on everyone she met — spirited, service-oriented, and always ready with a laugh. She taught us that serving others is one of the best ways to build community.

Naming our business after grandma O'Neal felt like the perfect way to honor that legacy while creating something new of our own. What began as a shared passion for crafting good drinks has turned into a family adventure. My husband and I bring the coffee know-how, and our daughter is the official lemonade maker and taste-tester — though honestly, she might be the most creative of the three of us.

For us, O’Neal Street isn’t just a name — it’s a feeling. It’s the kind of place where people gather, connect, and celebrate life’s moments both big and small. Whether we’re set up at a wedding, a work party, or right in your neighborhood, our goal is to make it easy for you to relax, enjoy, and share something special together.

So come share a drink, a smile, and a story. Meet us at O’Neal Street.

At O’Neal Street Coffee, we believe drinks bring people together.
